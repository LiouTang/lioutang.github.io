Research:

My current project focuses on Machine Unlearning (MU) and its related privacy and security attacks and defenses. MU has been widely adopted as a method to update ML models efficiently following users' requests to remove training data without retraining of the original ML model. We propose a behavior-based approach to MU as a counterpart to the gradient-based approaches of existing literature, by modeling the desired model behavior after unlearning through statistical methods, we try to provide more mathematically sound approach to MU.
