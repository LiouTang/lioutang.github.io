About:

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Pittsburgh under the guidance of Dr. James Joshi as part of the LERSAIS Lab. My research focus is on Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning (PPML), Agentic AI and Cryptography.

Research:

My current project concerns Machine Unlearning (MU) and its related privacy and security attacks and defenses. This project is currently funded by Cisco Research. I'm also exploring topics on Quantum Machine Learning (QML) and its security & privacy issues.
